our process

We design user friendly, eye catching objective oriented original websites, applications and digital content that are coded to meet industry standards, that are robust and reliable.

Discovery

We first seek to understand why the idea was conceived, by inquiring into its identity and philosophy. This discovery helps us identify the perception that needs to be expressed in its purest sense.

Planning

Once the brand identity is identified, we plan on What is needed to be expressed. We chart out a plan on what the various components are, which put together could actualise the larger idea.

Prototype

To ignite the process, we seek a spark on how we could manifest the idea on a larger canvas A prototype of the larger vision serves as a guiding light that shows us what lies ahead

Design

Once a concencus is reached on the prototype, we let the creative mind steer the chariot. The intent is to express the design beyond visualization and convey the values and philosophy of the brand

Development

With the visual structure in place, the logical mind is unleashed to put together the functional aspects of the art. The process of development involves intense technical work, which is no less a form of art in itself.

Delivery

Stitching together creative art and functional logic, a manifestation of the idea is produced. The process of delivery involves a deep commitment and a delicate balance of efficiency and elegance.
